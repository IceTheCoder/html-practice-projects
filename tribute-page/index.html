Steve Jobs Tribute Page

Steve Jobs (24 February 1955 - 5 October 2011) was a genius American inventor, best known for co-founding Apple; he also co-founded Pixar and founded NeXT. He was a pioneer of the computer revolution in the 1970s and '80s, along with Steve Wozniak.

Short timeline

Apple

Founding Apple

Steve Jobs co-founded Apple on April 1st, 1976 with Steve Wozniak to develop and sell Wozniak's Apple I personal computer, which he had finished in March 1976. Steve Wozniak initially hesitated to sell his computer, but eventually accepted Steve Jobs's idea to market and sell Apple I. Their operations initially started in Steve Jobs's bedroom and later moved to the garage.
