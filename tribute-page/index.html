Steve Jobs Tribute Page

Steve Jobs (24 February 1955 - 5 October 2011) was an American genius inventor, best known for co-founding Apple; he also co-founded Pixar and founded NeXT. He was a pioneer of the computer revolution in the 1970s and '80s, along with Steve Wozniak.
